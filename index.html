My Sketches for the News App on iPhones


I changed several things about this app. For one, the top stories were a lot smaller on the normal app so they could fit more on the screen. I made them bigger so it is easier to click on the one the user wants. I also made the buttons bigger and wider on the 'News +' page so it is also easier to click on. Finally, I organized the 'Following' page better so it was easier to navigate through it.
